## 1. Executive Summary

This proposal covers the urgent design, configuration and implementation of an AI-powered WhatsApp assistant for Solver Accommodations.

The assistant will support two different types of users:

- people looking for accommodation; and
- landlords or property owners who want to offer, list or advertise properties.

Because these two audiences have different needs, questions, risks and commercial objectives, the assistant will not be built as a generic chatbot. It will use a dual-flow structure, with separate conversation logic for tenants/guests and landlords/property owners.

The objective of this first phase is to create an initial MVP operational version that can receive enquiries, identify the user type, qualify the lead, collect essential information and escalate to a human representative when required.

Where WhatsApp/Meta/Twilio access is fully available and approved, the initial version will be connected to the WhatsApp channel. If third-party WhatsApp approval, number verification, provider access or platform availability is delayed, the Service Provider will deliver the AI workflows and integration-ready backend in a test/staging environment until the relevant third-party service becomes available.

This implementation is intended to improve first-response handling and lead qualification. It does not replace human supervision, legal review, property-management decisions or regulatory compliance obligations.

## 3. Tenant / Guest Workflow

The tenant/guest workflow will be designed to assist people looking for accommodation by collecting and organising key enquiry details, such as:

- type of stay or rental enquiry;
- preferred location;
- intended dates or moving timeframe;
- length of stay;
- number of guests or occupants;
- budget range, where applicable;
- property type or room requirements;
- reason for stay, where appropriate;
- viewing or availability enquiry;
- preferred contact route;
- relevant next steps.

The assistant may provide general guidance based on approved information supplied by the Client. Any property availability, pricing, eligibility or booking confirmation will depend on the data and operational process made available by the Client.

The assistant will not independently make binding rental, tenancy, legal, eligibility or financial decisions.

---

## 4. Landlord / Property Owner Workflow

The landlord/property owner workflow will be designed to assist users who want to offer, list or advertise a property.

The assistant may collect initial information such as:

- property type;
- property location;
- number of bedrooms or occupancy details;
- availability;
- whether the property is furnished;
- expected rental or short-stay terms;
- existing photographs or listing materials;
- preferred contact method;
- whether the owner needs help preparing a listing;
- relevant next steps for review by the Client.

The assistant may guide property owners through the initial enquiry process and escalate qualified leads to the Client’s team.

The assistant will not independently approve properties, verify ownership, conduct legal checks, create binding listings, set rental prices or confirm contractual terms.
